Data Diary With Chatbots


09.2016 _ 11.2016 / boston

How well can a chatbot converse with a human user?


By applying sentiment tagging to the conversations with four commercial chatbots (Siri, Alexa, Google Now, Microsoft xiaoIce ), this animated data visualization constructed an “empathy map” that grounded the idea for my M.F.A thesis which regards virtual personal assistant as an affective interface.

The chatbots engaged different techniques to provided empathetic responses to tones and emotions they detected from the user through the turn-taking conversations, though sometimes still failed as babblings of nonsense.











_ development tool:
AlchemyLanguage, node.js, jquery, d3.js
