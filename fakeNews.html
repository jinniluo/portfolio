Tracing the Twitter Bots


05.2017 _ now [ a work in progress ]




How can a seemingly human twitter user metamorphoze to a twitter bot? Are there any criterias existing yet that we could use to make the right judgement on the reliability of the information we ingested on a daily basis from social media?

This study matches the name and location that only appeared once from the voter records for 2017 presidential election with the twitter accounts that has identical registration information. By tracking the activities and links these accounts shared from several target sources over a period of time, some suspicious behavioral patterns can be identified from the visualization.

Below is a static data visualization that presents comparisons amount twitter accounts that are suspicious.
